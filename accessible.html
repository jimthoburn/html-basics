HTML starts out fast, responsive, simple and easy. You can make things faster, but not making them slower. You can make things responsive by not making them rigid. You can make things simple by not making them complicated. You can make things easy by not making them hard. Make the site that you want to use. ## Delegate to the browser and device By writing accessible HTML and using progressive enhancement, you delegate the feature support to the browser or device.
